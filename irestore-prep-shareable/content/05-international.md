## Regulatory Compliance Steps
<!-- id: intl-compliance -->

<p class="muted">High-level regulatory pathways by market. Each market requires specific planning around device classification, testing requirements, and timeline.</p>

<table>
  <tr><th>Market</th><th>Regulatory Body</th><th>Timeline</th><th>Key Requirements</th><th>Complexity</th></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>South Korea</strong></td><td>MFDS</td><td>6-12 months</td><td>Device classification, safety testing, Korean labeling, local authorized representative</td><td><span class="tag tag-warm">Moderate</span></td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Japan</strong></td><td>PMDA</td><td>12-36 months</td><td>DMAH partner required, Shonin approval, Japanese clinical data may be needed</td><td><span class="tag tag-red">High</span></td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>UK</strong></td><td>MHRA</td><td>6-12 months</td><td>UKCA marking (post-Brexit), device registration, UK Responsible Person</td><td><span class="tag tag-warm">Moderate</span></td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>EU</strong></td><td>EU MDR</td><td>12-18 months</td><td>CE marking, Notified Body assessment, EU Authorized Representative, technical file</td><td><span class="tag tag-red">High</span></td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Australia</strong></td><td>TGA</td><td>3-6 months</td><td>ARTG listing (low-risk devices), sponsor registration</td><td><span class="tag tag-purple">Low</span></td></tr>
</table>

<div class="tip"><strong>Sequencing recommendation:</strong> Australia TGA (quick win, existing distributor) → South Korea MFDS (largest TAM relative to timeline) → UK MHRA (English-speaking, Amazon infrastructure) → Japan PMDA (highest value but longest timeline). Run Australia and Korea in parallel to maximize speed.</div>

## Exit Value Connection
<!-- id: intl-exit-value -->

<div class="card">
  <div class="card-header">Why International Expansion Directly Increases Exit Value</div>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Unilever</strong> specifically seeks "US-based brands exportable to international markets" — international presence is a valuation lever, not just revenue</li>
    <li><strong>L'Oréal</strong> looks for "China and APAC growth potential" — even early APAC traction changes the narrative</li>
    <li><strong>Shiseido</strong> — Asia expansion synergies via their distribution network is a genuine value-add argument</li>
    <li><strong>CurrentBody</strong> (75% international) demonstrates that international mix drives public market premium</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Every international market opened before an exit process is a growth story the acquirer doesn't have to build themselves. It de-risks the acquisition and supports premium multiples.</p>
</div>
