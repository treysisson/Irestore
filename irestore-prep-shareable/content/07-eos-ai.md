## What This Is
<!-- id: eos-overview -->

<p>One of the things I've been building is a complete implementation of the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS) that runs through Claude — an AI assistant that manages the entire operating cadence for a company.</p>

<p>This isn't a concept or a pitch deck. It's working software that I use. Here's what it does:</p>

<div class="card">
  <div class="card-header">EOS Components Implemented</div>
  <table>
    <tr><th>EOS Tool</th><th>What It Does</th><th>How AI Runs It</th></tr>
    <tr><td><strong>Weekly L10 Meetings</strong></td><td>Structured 90-minute leadership meetings with segue, scorecard review, rock review, to-do review, IDS, and closing</td><td>AI facilitates the meeting structure, tracks time, captures decisions, and generates action items</td></tr>
    <tr><td><strong>Scorecard</strong></td><td>Weekly KPI tracking with targets, actuals, and trend analysis</td><td>Auto-pulls data where possible, flags off-track metrics, provides trend commentary</td></tr>
    <tr><td><strong>Rocks</strong></td><td>Quarterly priorities (3-7 per person) with on/off track status</td><td>Tracks progress, sends reminders, flags at-risk rocks before quarter end</td></tr>
    <tr><td><strong>Accountability Chart</strong></td><td>Clear roles, responsibilities, and reporting lines</td><td>Maintains the org structure, identifies gaps, tracks seat changes</td></tr>
    <tr><td><strong>Vision/Traction Organizer (VTO)</strong></td><td>Company vision, 10-year target, 3-year picture, 1-year plan</td><td>Keeps strategic alignment visible, tracks progress against milestones</td></tr>
    <tr><td><strong>IDS (Identify, Discuss, Solve)</strong></td><td>Structured problem-solving process</td><td>Maintains issues list, facilitates IDS sessions, tracks resolution</td></tr>
    <tr><td><strong>To-Dos</strong></td><td>7-day action items from L10s</td><td>Tracks completion, carries forward, identifies chronic open items</td></tr>
    <tr><td><strong>People Analyzer</strong></td><td>Right people, right seats evaluation</td><td>Structured assessment against core values + GWC (Get it, Want it, Capacity)</td></tr>
    <tr><td><strong>Process Documentation</strong></td><td>Core processes documented and followed by all</td><td>Generates and maintains process docs, tracks adherence</td></tr>
  </table>
</div>

## Why This Matters for iRESTORE
<!-- id: eos-irestore -->

<div class="answer-block">
  <p><strong>The operational challenge:</strong> iRESTORE is scaling from $90M to $150M+ with 3 simultaneous executive departures, 6-7 new product launches, and expansion into new channels (QVC, B2B) and categories (panels, belts). That level of complexity requires a structured operating system — not just good intentions.</p>
</div>

<h3>What This Could Look Like at iRESTORE</h3>

<table>
  <tr><th>Application</th><th>Impact</th></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Weekly leadership meeting</strong></td><td>Structured L10 with Kevin and direct reports. Issues surfaced systematically, not ad hoc. Scorecard reviewed weekly — everyone knows the numbers.</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Quarterly rocks</strong></td><td>Each product launch, international market entry, and operational initiative tracked as a rock with clear owner and milestones. At-risk items flagged early.</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>CX analytics</strong></td><td>All calls already recorded + transcribed. AI analysis pipeline identifies return drivers, customer sentiment trends, and coaching opportunities — the exact gap the departing CX Director left.</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Demand planning</strong></td><td>AI-assisted forecasting across 52+ SKUs, 5 channels, and seasonal patterns. Coordinates with multiple Shenzhen manufacturers.</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Cross-functional visibility</strong></td><td>Kevin gets a dashboard, not a messenger. The "Daniel problem" — everything flowing through one person — is solved by systems, not headcount.</td></tr>
</table>

<div class="tip"><strong>The meta-point:</strong> The fact that this document exists — researched, synthesized, and formatted by AI — is itself a demonstration of the approach. AI doesn't replace judgment; it removes the friction between having an idea and executing on it.</div>
