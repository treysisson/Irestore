## Role Overview
<!-- id: jd-overview -->

<h2>COO (Integrator) — iRESTORE</h2>
      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">Position Details</div>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Position:</strong> COO (Integrator)</li>
          <li><strong>Reports To:</strong> CEO/Founder (Kevin Chen)</li>
          <li><strong>Location:</strong> Orange County, CA (On-site/Hybrid) — LA-based strongly preferred; relocation assistance available</li>
          <li><strong>Compensation:</strong> Competitive base + performance bonus + equity</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <h3>The Opportunity</h3>
      <p>As the Operational Strategist and true Integrator, you'll be the bridge between vision and execution. You'll partner directly with Kevin to translate strategic vision into operational execution plans. Your role is to connect all the dots across supply chain, inventory, finance, customer experience, and team operations — ensuring that strategy becomes reality through clear execution and operational excellence.</p>
      <p><strong>This role frees the CEO</strong> to focus on innovation (product + AI), sales, and big relationships — while you own the operational engine.</p>

## Key Responsibilities
<!-- id: jd-responsibilities -->

<h2>Key Responsibilities</h2>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">Strategy Translation & Execution Planning</div>
        <ul>
          <li>Translate CEO's strategic vision into clear operational execution plans each department can work against</li>
          <li>Connect strategic goals to operational KPIs, budgets, and timelines across all functions</li>
          <li>Work with CFO and Controller to break down financial targets into departmental execution plans</li>
          <li>Ensure alignment through regular operational reviews and dashboards</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">Strategy-to-Execution Translation in Practice</div>
        <p>Strategy translation is central to the COO role. The following examples illustrate how strategic directives become operational execution plans:</p>
        <table>
          <tr><th style="width:35%">Strategic Direction</th><th>Operational Translation</th></tr>
          <tr>
            <td>Expand into retail (Sephora/Ulta for skincare)</td>
            <td>90-day retail readiness plan: retail-spec packaging redesign (wk 1–3), MAP policy finalized (wk 2), sell sheets + planogram built (wk 3–4), broker/rep firm shortlisted (wk 4–6), buyer meetings booked (wk 8–12). Track: first PO target Q3. Cross-functional ownership across marketing (creative), ops (packaging/fulfillment), and finance (margin modeling by channel).</td>
          </tr>
          <tr>
            <td>Internationalization expansion</td>
            <td>Phased rollout: Phase 1 (60 days) — Amazon UK + DE via FBA Europe, translated listings, VAT registration. Phase 2 (6 mo) — Korea via distributor model, MFDS registration filed. Phase 3 (12 mo) — Japan PMDA pathway. Amazon logistics coordination with Brandon; regulatory specialist hired or contracted for Korea/Japan. Target: $2M international revenue in first 12 months.</td>
          </tr>
          <tr>
            <td>Build beauty brand positioning and recurring revenue</td>
            <td>Subscription consumables initiative: post-purchase email flows targeting 600K+ device owners with bundled serum/supplement offers (subscribe-and-save at 15% discount). Operations implements recurring fulfillment workflow in Fulfill.io; supplier volume pricing negotiated for 3x consumable scaling. Marketing designs lifecycle email sequence. Finance models LTV impact at multiple conversion scenarios. Target: 10% of device buyers converting to $40/mo subscription within 12 months.</td>
          </tr>
          <tr>
            <td>Launch sub-$200 wand for mass retail</td>
            <td>Target/Walmart readiness: retail unit economics finalized at $199 MSRP (validate 40%+ retailer margin with COGS), retail-ready packaging sourced, anti-theft fixtures designed, inventory positioned for 500-store test. Operations: separate retail fulfillment workflow from DTC/Amazon. Marketing: co-op advertising budget + in-store demo plan. Target: $500K/mo run rate within 6 months of placement.</td>
          </tr>
          <tr>
            <td>Build toward 2–4 year, $500M+ exit</td>
            <td>Exit readiness roadmap: (1) Clean financials — audited GAAP statements by EOY, owner add-back normalization, (2) SOP documentation for operations continuity, (3) Revenue diversification — reduce DTC/Amazon concentration below 50% via retail + international, (4) Recurring revenue expansion (subscriptions) for valuation multiple uplift, (5) Board-ready quarterly reporting package. Each workstream assigned to an owner with clear deadlines and dashboard metrics.</td>
          </tr>
        </table>
        <p><strong>The fundamental pattern:</strong> Strategic directives are translated into detailed operational plans with clear ownership, timelines, resource assignments, and measurable outcomes. Vision becomes execution.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">Operational Leadership</div>
        <ul>
          <li>Lead teams across supply chain, fulfillment, customer service, and quality assurance</li>
          <li>Build and document SOPs to systematize operations and reduce founder dependency</li>
          <li>Run operational meetings and drive decisions (CEO input on major decisions)</li>
          <li>Drive margin improvement through supplier negotiations, logistics optimization, process efficiency</li>
          <li>Support new product launches with operational readiness planning</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">Inventory Management & Forecasting</div>
        <ul>
          <li>Implement inventory management best practices and identify/setup appropriate systems</li>
          <li>Develop accurate forecasting models and streamline supply-demand balance</li>
          <li>Analyze inventory to identify overstock/understock issues and optimize stock rotation</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">ERP & Systems</div>
        <ul>
          <li>Maximize ERP system (Fulfil.io) through customization, optimization, and integration</li>
          <li>Build systems and automation to drive operational efficiency (including AI-powered workflows)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">Cross-Functional Integration</div>
        <ul>
          <li>Connect all dots across departments — break down silos</li>
          <li>Improve cross-functional communication</li>
          <li>Lead risk assessment, mitigation planning, and compliance</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">People & Culture</div>
        <ul>
          <li>Lead people operations with Chief of Staff: HR issues, team development for direct reports</li>
          <li>Partner with CEO on recruiting strategy and building A-player bench strength</li>
          <li>Champion company values: think big, proactive problem-solving, fail fast and learn</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">Financial Stewardship</div>
        <ul>
          <li>Own operational P&L and EBITDA targets</li>
          <li>Partner with CFO and Controller on forecasting, budgeting, and variance analysis</li>
          <li>Make data-driven decisions balancing growth investment with profitability</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
