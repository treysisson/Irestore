## How to Use in Interview
<!-- id: exit-interview -->

<ul>
        <li><strong>Don't bring up exit directly</strong> — Kevin wants a builder. But if asked about long-term vision, show you understand value creation levers.</li>
        <li><strong>Frame it as:</strong> "I think about operations through the lens of enterprise value — every margin point, every system, every new channel contributes to a valuation premium."</li>
        <li><strong>Reference CurrentBody:</strong> "If CurrentBody at £128M can IPO at £300M, iRESTORE at $150M+ with better margins should command a significant premium."</li>
        <li><strong>Your value as COO:</strong> Each gross margin point = $1.5M+ EBITDA at $150M rev. Operational systems de-risk for buyers. International expansion = growth runway. Channel diversification = reduces Amazon dependency risk.</li>
      </ul>
