  <tr><td><strong>Hooga Belt</strong></td><td>$279–$299</td><td>15,000mAh rechargeable battery. #1 battery-powered belt.</td><td><span class="tag tag-warm">Medium</span></td></tr>

Zero belts connect to an app. Amazon is wide open for a premium brand. iRESTORE enters with structural advantages across every dimension.</div>
