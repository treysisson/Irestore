      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">Structural Considerations</div>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Model A (Marketing under COO):</strong> All department heads report to COO. This creates clean P&L accountability. Requires tight coordination between marketing and ops for channel-mix optimization.</li>
          <li><strong>Model B (Marketing direct to CEO):</strong> Kevin retains direct control of growth strategy and brand direction. COO maintains operational mandate across supply chain, finance, and customer experience.</li>
          <li><strong>Model C (Dual reporting):</strong> Marketing reports operationally to COO (budgets, headcount, metrics) and strategically to CEO for creative direction and brand strategy. This structure is common in CEO-Visionary / COO-Integrator setups.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="tip"><strong>EOS context:</strong> In the Entrepreneurial Operating System, department heads typically report to the Integrator role. However, CEO-Visionaries often maintain hands-on involvement with marketing and brand strategy. Dotted-line reporting (solid operational line to COO, strategic input from CEO) balances these needs.</div>

## Organizational Operating Model
<!-- id: org-structure -->

<ul>
        <li><strong>Lean C-suite structure:</strong> Kevin serves as CEO. Organizational structure focuses on VP/Director level roles across functional areas. The company is exploring addition of C-level operational leadership.</li>
        <li><strong>EOS implementation:</strong> The company has adopted the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS) framework. Weekly L10 leadership meetings occur Wednesday mornings. Implementation is advancing across goal-setting, accountability, and team alignment.</li>
        <li><strong>Hybrid work model:</strong> Operations, warehouse, and finance functions operate in-person at La Mirada headquarters. Growth & Marketing operates fully remote. Leadership offices at La Mirada support periodic in-person strategy and operations oversight.</li>
        <li><strong>Product development involvement:</strong> Despite recent hire of Head of Product Development (Longchuan Huang), CEO maintains active involvement in innovation strategy and product direction.</li>
        <li><strong>B2B expansion:</strong> New B2B sales division launched to target clinic and salon markets. Higher-priced equipment (~$10K) targeted at professional end-users, sourced through Shenzhen manufacturing network.</li>
      </ul>
