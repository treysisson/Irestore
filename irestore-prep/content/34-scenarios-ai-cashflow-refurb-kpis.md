<h4>What to Say About Cash Flow</h4>
<div class="card">
<ul>
<li><strong>"The negative cash conversion cycle is your secret weapon."</strong> You collect cash from DTC customers instantly, Amazon in 14 days, but don't pay your factory for 150 days. At $90M revenue, that means you're always sitting on $15-20M of customer cash before supplier payments come due. Most DTC brands are starving for working capital — you have excess.</li>
<li><strong>"The risk isn't cash — it's inventory allocation."</strong> With multiple new launches in Q2, the danger is over-committing to inventory on products with unknown demand while under-investing in proven winners. I'd set a hard cap: no single new product PO exceeds $500K until we have 90 days of sell-through data.</li>
<li><strong>"The fractional CFO handles the mechanics. I'd own the strategy."</strong> Cash flow isn't just an accounting exercise — it's a strategic weapon. Example: if you have $15M in excess working capital, do you invest in B2B sales acceleration, east coast 3PL, or marketing? That's a COO decision, not a CFO decision.</li>
</ul>
</div>

</ul></li>

</ol>
