## CRITICAL OPERATIONAL REQUIREMENTS

| Requirement | Who Needs It | What It Means |
|---|---|---|
| **EDI Compliance** | Ulta (30 days), Nordstrom (60 days) | Electronic order system = non-negotiable. Cost: $500-1K/month |
| **Retail-Ready Packaging** | Walmart, Target, all retail | Different from DTC. Cost: $25-50K redesign + $1.50/unit |
| **MAP (Minimum Advertised Price) Policy** | All channels | Protect retail margins by controlling advertised pricing. Do this WEEK 1 |
| **Anti-Theft Tagging** | High-value items (>$150) | Sensormatic requirement for all retail placement |
| **Insurance** | All retail over $100K GMV | $1M liability minimum. Cost: $2-4K/year |
| **Fill Rate SLA** | Walmart, Target, Ulta | 95%+ on-time delivery. Chargebacks if you miss. |
