WHAT NOT TO DO IN THE INTERVIEW
================================================================================

❌ Say "Sephora is a game-changer"
   → It's not. Walmart is the game-changer.

❌ Say "Retail is lower margin but we'll make it up in volume"
   → That's not the strategy. Walmart IS good margin ($35/unit).

❌ Assume timeline: "We can get Sephora approval in 90 days"
   → Approval by Month 4, first POs by Month 5-6.

❌ Ignore the SMU strategy: "We'll just use the same SKU everywhere"
   → That creates channel conflict. SMU (exclusive colors) prevents that.

❌ Underestimate working capital: "Retail is just a sales channel"
   → Walmart test = $315K inventory + $200K packaging/co-op + $200K ops = $700K+

❌ Overpromise: "I'll have Walmart national by year-end"
   → Realistic: 500-store test by Month 6, national by Month 12-15.
