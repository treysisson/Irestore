## Return Rate Optimization
<!-- id: opps-returns -->

<div class="metric-row">
  <div class="metric-card"><div class="metric-value">10%</div><div class="metric-label">Current DTC Returns</div></div>
  <div class="metric-card"><div class="metric-value">14-15%</div><div class="metric-label">Current Amazon Returns</div></div>
  <div class="metric-card"><div class="metric-value">30%</div><div class="metric-label">Costco Lifetime Returns</div></div>
  <div class="metric-card accent"><div class="metric-value">&lt;8%</div><div class="metric-label">Target (Kevin's Goal)</div></div>
</div>

<p>iRESTORE already dropped DTC returns from 17% to 10% through phone calls and negotiation scripts. All calls are recorded and transcribed — but there's <strong>no AI analysis</strong> yet.</p>

<table>
  <tr><th>Scenario</th><th>Assumption</th><th>Annual Impact (at $150M rev)</th></tr>
  <tr><td>DTC: 10% → 8%</td><td>AI-driven return analysis identifies top return drivers. Targeted interventions on product education, expectation setting, proactive outreach.</td><td><strong>~$3.0M recovered</strong> (2pts × $150M × 67% DTC mix × blended margin)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Amazon: 15% → 12%</td><td>Better listing content, bundle strategy reduces "wrong product" returns. Enhanced A+ content sets expectations.</td><td><strong>~$1.0M recovered</strong></td></tr>
  <tr><td>Costco: 30% → 25%</td><td>Costco-specific SKUs with right-sized specs + pricing reduce "returns as product trial" behavior.</td><td><strong>~$0.75M recovered</strong></td></tr>
</table>

<div class="tip"><strong>The unlock:</strong> All the call data exists — recorded, transcribed, sitting in a system. Nobody is analyzing it at scale. AI analysis of return call transcripts would immediately surface the top 5-10 reasons people return, enabling targeted product/process fixes rather than blanket retention scripts.</div>

## CX Team Restructuring
<!-- id: opps-cx -->

<p>25-person CX team. The departing director was let go specifically for not implementing AI. This is an immediate opportunity.</p>

<table>
  <tr><th>Initiative</th><th>Impact</th></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>AI-assisted ticket triage</strong></td><td>Auto-categorize, route, and draft responses for routine inquiries. Reduce first-response time.</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Call transcript analysis</strong></td><td>Automated sentiment analysis, return driver identification, coaching recommendations at scale.</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Proactive outreach automation</strong></td><td>Identify at-risk customers (low engagement, approaching return window) for proactive intervention.</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Knowledge base + self-service</strong></td><td>AI-powered FAQ, troubleshooting guides, video recommendations to deflect routine tickets.</td></tr>
</table>

<p class="muted">Potential to improve service quality while right-sizing the team. Not about cutting heads — about redeploying capacity toward higher-value customer interactions (retention, upsell, feedback collection).</p>
