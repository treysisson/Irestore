## Structural Notes
<!-- id: org-structure -->

<ul>
        <li><strong>No C-suite:</strong> Kevin is the only executive. Everyone else is VP/Director level. The COO would be the first C-level hire.</li>
        <li><strong>Kevin's Role: Value Creation; COO's Role: Value Protection</strong> — From the Mar 13 interview: Kevin owns marketing, sales, product innovation, and distribution (the "value creation" side). COO owns operations, finance, CX, and supply chain (the "value protection" side). <strong>This is NOT a traditional V/I model where everything flows through one person.</strong> Kevin and COO have distinct domains. Kevin stays hands-on with growth and innovation; COO ensures execution and efficiency.</li>
        <li><strong>EOS at ~60%:</strong> They've adopted the Entrepreneurial Operating System but aren't fully implemented. L10 leadership meeting happens Wednesday mornings. Completing EOS implementation is a key deliverable for the COO.</li>
